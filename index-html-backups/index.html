Biography

Who Am I?

Dr.Mrs Kalpana S Thakre is a Professor of Department of Information Technology of Sinhgad College of Engineering affiliated to Pune University where she is working since 2007. She received a BE(CSE) in 1993, and ME(CSE) in 2006 from Nagpur University. She received her PhD in Computer Science and Engineering from Swami RamanandTirthMarathwada University,Nandedin Feb2016. During 1994 to 2006, she was Assistant professor in Nagpur University.

Her research interests basicallycenter on improving the design and performance of image and video retrieval systems using spatiotemporal features in compressed domian.In addition to this,she has alsoworked on computer vision, database, and user interface techniques and Mining and Analyzing Social Media Data for Interpreting Student Learning Exepirences and brain seekness of teenagers.

She is also working and teaching Database Management Systems, especiallyquery processing involving structured (such as relational and object-oriented data) and unstructured data (such as text, images, and video) in centralized and distributed environments.

She was the Session chair, publication chair and reviewer as well for many InternationalConferences and Journals. Recently she was awarded as a “Distinguished Women in IT” by VIWA-2018 (Venus International Women’s Award).

She has published over 50 papers in various journals and conference proceedings. She is also guiding many of undergraduate and postgraduate students for their academic projects and thesis writing.She is a life member of ACM,CSI and ISTE.
